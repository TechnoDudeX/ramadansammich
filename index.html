🥪 What We Do

Assembling meals, building community

Ramadan Sammich is a community-driven initiative that assembles and distributes meals to local shelters in the GTA every Ramadan.

Volunteers gather weekly to prepare and deliver 200 meals per run, fostering community, support, and giving back during the holiest month of the year.
