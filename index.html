01 — What we do

Assembling meals.
Building community. 💛

A bag of food is small. A bag of food shows up every week, for thirteen years, and it becomes something else entirely — a habit of care 🤲, a piece of a community, a way of belonging to your city.

01

🛒 Prep

Volunteers shop, slice, and lay out 200 portions on long folding tables — bread, bologna, cheese, the works.

02

🥪 Pack

Assembly-line style: a sammich gets built, sealed, paired with a clementine, juice, and snack — all in a paper bag.

03

🚐 Deliver

Cars load up and fan out across the GTA, dropping bags at shelters before iftar. Same time, same care, every week.

04 — Our story

🌟 From 50 sammiches to thousands.

Thirteen years of weekly runs, late nights of prep, and a community that keeps showing up.

2012

The First Run

It all started with 50 sammiches and a small group of friends with one good idea.

2016

Word Spreads

Volunteers from across the GTA join in. Shelters request regular drop-offs.

2020

Adapt & Persist

Pandemic year. Contactless prep, masked deliveries, more meals than ever.

2025

Stronger Together

200 meals every run, every week of Ramadan, across the whole region.
