🥪 What We Do

Assembling meals, building community

Ramadan Sammich is a community-driven initiative that assembles and distributes meals to local shelters in the GTA every Ramadan.

Volunteers gather weekly to prepare and deliver 200 meals per run, fostering community, support, and giving back during the holiest month of the year.

🌙 Our Story

From 50 sammiches to thousands

2012

The First Run

It all started with just 50 sammiches and a small group of friends wanting to make a difference during Ramadan.

2016

Growing Momentum

Word spread across the GTA. More volunteers joined, more shelters reached, more smiles shared.

2020

Adapting & Persisting

Even through a global pandemic, the community found safe ways to keep distributing meals to those in need.

2025

Stronger Than Ever

Now delivering 200 meals per run with a passionate community of volunteers. The mission continues to grow.
